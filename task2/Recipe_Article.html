Chapathi

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water and mix until a soft dough is formed.
  3. Roll out the dough into a round.
  4. Heat a tawa or pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Place the chapati on the hot tawa.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook further.
  7. Gently press down on the chapati with a spatula to help it puff up.

Egg Burji

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tomato

  • Bell peppers
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Butter or oil
  • Ginger-garlic paste

Instructions

  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.

  4. Add chopped tomatoes, cook until soft, then add ginger-garlic paste and spices.
  5. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble them until fully cooked.
  7. Add garam masala and fresh coriander leaves.
