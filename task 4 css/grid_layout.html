Pamper your skin with this ultra-hydrating face mask! Rose petals are rich in antioxidants that help fight dullness, while honey provides deep nourishment and antibacterial benefits. Simply blend fresh rose petals with a tablespoon of raw honey, apply to clean skin, and leave for 15 minutes before rinsing for a natural glow.

Chamomile & Oatmeal Scrub for Gentle Exfoliation

Exfoliate without irritation using this gentle homemade scrub. Chamomile reduces redness and soothes sensitive skin, while oatmeal provides mild exfoliation to remove dead skin cells. Grind oats into a fine powder, mix with brewed chamomile tea, and gently massage onto damp skin for a soft, smooth complexion.
